Purpose

In an emergency, the most valuable resource is often the person already standing next to you.
SH✡MER turns that instinct into simple, reliable infrastructure: one tap fires a loud alarm, shares your live location with people you trust, and gives you direct access to the right Israeli emergency number.

Tone & Values

Calm. Credible. Protective.

Plain-spoken. Never alarmist. We earn trust by under-promising and being scrupulously honest about what the tool can and cannot do today.

Honesty as a feature

We clearly show what is real right now (alarm, map, Dead Man’s Switch, same-device guardian) versus what requires the backend phase. No fear-selling.

Locally rooted

Hebrew-first with full RTL. Correct Israeli authorities (100 / 101 / 102). 6 languages. Built for the realities of life here.

Accessible guardianship

Free core protection. Works as a PWA on any phone. No app store. No tracking. Your data stays on your device. Privacy-first by design.

What SH✡MER actually does today

Instant loud SOS alarm

Three real WebAudio sirens that fire on your device immediately. Engineered to deter and call for help around you.

Live safety map + GPS

Real Leaflet map with your location. Honest aggregate data from CBS and Israel Police (cited, never fabricated incidents).

Guardians you choose

Share live location with parents or partners. Real between browser tabs today. Cross-device in the backend phase.

Dead Man’s Switch + Shake

Set a timer while hiking or swimming alone. Confirm you’re OK or an SOS fires. Hard shake also triggers.

What still needs the backend
