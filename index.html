What actually works today

Real loud alarm

Three distinct WebAudio sirens — police, air-raid, deterrent. Fires instantly on your device. Designed to be frightening.

Live interactive map

Leaflet + real OpenStreetMap. Your location + category-coded safety data (public CBS & Police sources, cited).

Dead Man's Switch

Set an interval. Confirm you're OK or an SOS fires automatically. Perfect for hiking or swimming alone.

Guardian pairing

Invite a parent or partner. Real live location sharing between browser tabs today. Cross-device coming with backend.

Shake + photo reports

Hard shake triggers SOS. Take a photo + precise GPS for incidents. Everything stays on your device.

6 languages, full RTL

Hebrew, English, العربية, Русский, አማርኛ, Français. The entire interface localizes correctly.
