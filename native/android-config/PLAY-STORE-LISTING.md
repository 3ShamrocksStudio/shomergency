## Full description
SH✡MER turns your community into a safety network.

When you feel unsafe, one tap sends an SOS — with your live location — straight to your personal circle of guardians. No call center, no delay. The people who care about you see exactly where you are and can act immediately.

**How it works**
• Add guardians — family, friends, neighbors. They're invited by SMS and confirm with a secure match code.
• When you need help, press SOS. An alarm that can't be silenced fires, and your location reaches every guardian at once.
• Share your location live — but only when you choose to. You're always in control.
• See a live map of security and medical events near you, in real time.
• Built-in emergency guide — CPR, choking, bleeding, stroke — available even without internet.

**Your privacy is the foundation**
Your location is shared only with the guardians you approve, only when you allow it. Nothing is sold, nothing is public. Safety without surveillance.

**Free for citizens**
SH✡MER is free to use. Because safety shouldn't have a paywall.

Citizens for citizens.
